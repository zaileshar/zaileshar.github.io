Zailesh A R — VLSI Design Engineer

Zailesh A R (also written Zailesh AR) is a VLSI design engineer and M.Tech VLSI Design student at VIT, currently interning in circuit design at Intel. He works across the RTL-to-GDSII flow — RTL design in Verilog, logic synthesis, static timing analysis, custom CMOS and analog IC layout, low-power design, FPGA prototyping and device-level TCAD simulation. He holds a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Government Model Engineering College.
